Brand Idea

What does the company do, or offer?

This website is designed for a furniture company. The name of the furniture company is IDLE FURNITURE. This company is inspired by Neri & Hu Studio. This studio designed lots of unique products. There are lots of idle studios that designed lots of creative furniture. I decided to make a website about furniture companies, which will contain as many interesting and unique furniture items as possible in recent years.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

My target users are people who like creative products. These people should not only care about the functionality of the furniture or the product but also pay attention to the meaning behind the products (furniture) and what the designer tries to express through the products.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

There are already many furniture companies on the market, but these companies usually focus on the use of furniture. For example, IKEA's core philosophy is cheap and easy to assemble furniture. In my opinion, there are only a few furniture companies on the market that sell independent designer brands, especially fewer companies that sell many idle design studios' products at the same time. With the development of technology, people's living standards have become higher. Many young people pay more attention to the meaning of the product and the design of the product when choosing a product. So, in my opinion, my furniture company is very attractive to these people.
